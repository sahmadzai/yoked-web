What

Yoked is a workout journal designed to track your weight training progress effortlessly.

Why

To offer a simplified, journal-like experience without the complexity or paywalls common in other fitness tracking apps.

Goals

Our goal is to deliver a minimalistic, user-friendly app that provides clear visualizations of your progress, all at an affordable price.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I save my timers?

Yes, you can save your timers for future use and easily access them from your saved timers list.

What type of workouts can I use Yoked for?

Yoked is great for a variety of workouts, including Functional Fitness, HIIT, Tabata, Circuit Training, Boxing, Running, and more.

Are notifications required for Yoked to run in the background?

No, Yoked runs efficiently in the background without the need for notifications, ensuring uninterrupted workout tracking.
