What

Yoked is a workout journal designed to track your weight training progress effortlessly.

Why

To offer a simplified, journal-like experience without the complexity or paywalls common in other fitness tracking apps.

Goals

Our goal is to deliver a minimalistic, user-friendly app that provides clear visualizations of your progress, all at an affordable price.

Features

Log Your Workouts

Easily log your workouts and track your progress over time. Yoked makes it easy to record your exercises, sets, reps, and weights.

Track Your Progress

See how you are progressing over time with detailed graphs and statistics. Yoked helps you stay motivated and reach your fitness goals.

Set New Goals

Set new fitness goals and track your progress as you work towards them. Yoked helps you stay focused and motivated.
